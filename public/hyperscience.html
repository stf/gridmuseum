Hyper Science Cooking, In Your Living Room and Mine

Our Lesson Series:

Each lesson will focus on specific skills while teaching you, top to bottom, how to make a single delicious, healthy, multi-course meal. Throughout the course of the series, all of the basics needed for confident, creative, nourishing home chefery will be addressed, including:

1.) How to plan meals &emdash; balancing tasty, easy, and healthy

2.) Shopping decisions &emdash; what to buy organic, what to buy in bulk, and how to cope with the tyranny of choice

3.) How to organize food preparation &emdash; learn to love your 'mise en place'

4.) 4) Cooking techniques &emdash; how to wash, chop, cut, dice, what oils to use, and how and when to turn up the heat

Lesson 1

Lesson 1 focuses on shopping decisions like when to buy organic and what to buy in bulk. You'll learn the basics of how to wash, chop, cut, dice, what oils to use, and how and when to turn up the heat.
