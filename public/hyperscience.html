Hyper Science Cooking: In Your Living Room and Mine

Our Lesson Series:

Each lesson will focus on specific skills while teaching you, top to bottom, how to make a single delicious, healthy, multi-course meal. Throughout the course of the series, all of the basics needed for confident, creative, nourishing home chefery will be addressed, including:

1.) How to plan meals — balancing tasty, easy, and healthy

2.) Shopping decisions — what to buy organic, what to buy in bulk, and how to cope with the tyranny of choice

3.) How to organize food preparation — learn to love your 'mise en place'

4.) 4) Cooking techniques — how to wash, chop, cut, dice, what oils to use, and how and when to turn up the heat

Most importantly, we explain the science behind your food — how protein is broken down, how sauces thicken and how to retain nutrient rich food throughout the cooking process.

Every class comes equipped with wine or whisky (Templeton Rye, Red Breast, etc.) and everyone goes home with at least two servings of every dish.
